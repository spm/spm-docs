# MRI Physics Primer

## Course programme

This is a sample programme of the [MRI Physics Primer](./index.md). The course will consist of lectures introducing the fundamentals of MRI and conclude with an interactive quiz.  

Slides from a previous edition of the course are linked on the programme. 

**Faculty:** [Dr Yael Balbastre](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/61880-yael-balbastre), [Prof Martina Callaghan](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/36016-martina-callaghan), [Dr Nadège Corbin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=TjIzldkAAAAJ&hl=fr), [Dr Shokoufeh Golshani](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Shokoufeh-Golshani-2), [Dr Nadine Graedel](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/65628-nadine-graedel), Benjamin James.
